# Privacy Policy for Sentō

### 1. Data Collection

Sentō collects no personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no telemetry.

### 2. API Keys

Your API keys for OpenAI, Gemini, and Grok are stored locally on your device using `chrome.storage.local`. They are never sent to any server. They are only used to authenticate your requests directly to the AI provider you selected.

### 3. Selected Text

When you select text and click a rewrite template, the selected content is sent directly from your browser to the AI provider you selected (OpenAI, Google, or xAI) via their official APIs. Sentō is not involved in that transfer and does not store the content.

### 4. Third-Party Services

By using Sentō, you interact with third-party providers. Review their privacy policies:

- [OpenAI](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy/)
- [Google AI](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms)
- [xAI](https://x.ai/legal/privacy-policy/)

### 5. Open Source

Sentō is open source. You can review the full code at [github.com/artttj/sento](https://github.com/artttj/sento). Report issues or questions in the [issue tracker](https://github.com/artttj/sento/issues).
