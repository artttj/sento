Privacy & Data Notice

When rewriting text, the selected content is sent directly to your chosen AI provider. Please note:

  • No Storage: This extension stores nothing on any server. No content, no API keys — everything stays on your device.
  • Data Transfer: Your selected text leaves your device and is processed by the AI provider (OpenAI, Google, or xAI).
  • Personal Info: Avoid rewriting text that contains sensitive health, financial, or private credentials.
  • Your Responsibility: You are responsible for ensuring compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable data laws.

Help

How to use the rewrite bubble and keyboard shortcuts.

How it works

  1. Select text inside any editable field (textarea or contenteditable).
  2. The rewrite bubble appears below your selection with four templates: Fix, Pro, Mine, and Trim.
  3. Click a template. The text gets rewritten by your chosen AI provider.
  4. Review the preview, then click Apply to replace the original text. Skip this step by enabling Force Insert in General settings.

Rewrite templates

Fix
Fix grammar, spelling, punctuation, and clarity. Keeps the original meaning and tone.
Pro
Rewrite in a polished professional tone. Concise, courteous, specific.
Mine
Your own template with a custom rewrite instruction. Configure it in General settings.
Trim
Make the text significantly shorter while keeping key points and action items.

Shortcuts

Shift + Click
Force apply
Hold Shift when clicking a template to skip the preview and apply the rewrite directly. Enable Force Insert in General to always skip preview.

Tips

  • System prompt: the default prompt humanizes all rewrites. You can change it in General settings.
  • Long text: selections over 12,000 characters get truncated. Keep selections focused for better results.
  • Multiple providers: configure all three providers in AI Connections, then switch between them in General settings.

Privacy

How Sentō handles your data.

Data Collection

Sentō collects no personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no telemetry.

API Keys

Your API keys are stored locally using chrome.storage.local. They are never sent to any server — only used to authenticate your requests directly to the AI provider you selected.

Selected Text

When you rewrite text, the selected content is sent directly from your browser to the AI provider. Sentō is not involved in that transfer and does not store it.

Third-Party Services

By using Sentō you interact with third-party AI providers. Review their privacy policies:

  • OpenAI
  • Google AI
  • xAI (Grok)
